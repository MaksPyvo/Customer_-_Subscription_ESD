Community Food Partners (CFP)

CFP registers clients in the program. - F2Fes must be able to acquire this information to define and manage user access, orders, etc.

AgNet

AgNet is the system that sources and provides food for F2Fes to order and have shipped to the warehouse provider. - F2Fes must be able to interact with AgNet and coordinate shipments to the warehouse so that food is available for ordering.

Customer Inventory System (CIS)

The CIS at the warehouse interacts with F2Fes to coordinate inventory levels and to package orders for pickup and delivery. - Clients enrolled in F2F use the online ordering system our class will develop to pick and request an order delivery.

Online Delivery Services (ODS)

ODS is queued by F2Fes to pickup a shipment from the Warehouse and deliver it to the registered address for the F2F enrolled client. - F2Fes coordinates the delivery through ODS integration, and updates both CIS and CFP when the delivery is completed.
